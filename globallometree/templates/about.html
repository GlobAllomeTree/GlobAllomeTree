About GlobAllomeTree

GlobAllomeTree is the international platform for tree allometric equations. GlobAllomeTree was created in 2013 in response to the increasing demand in assessment of the forest resources and builds on the convening role and technical expertise of the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (FAO), the Centre de coopération internationale en recherche agronomique pour le développement (CIRAD), the Università degli Studi della Tuscia (UNITUS). GlobAllomeTree currently provides allometric equations for 57 countries in the world, from boreal forest to tropical rainforests. The products provided by GlobAllomeTree are continuously updated and approved by experts.

Users are welcome to provide comments and suggestions to improve the database and the software and facilitate their use.

Users can contact the GlobAllomeTree Administrator at: globallometree@fao.org
